What?

Truce is an online B2B curated marketplace for fruits and vegetables. We help wholesale buyers of fruits and vegetables to reach out to farmers across India to order products at better prices.

Who is Truce app for?

Truce app is for wholesalers and traders looking to buy fruits and vegetables directly from farmers across India.

What all can I buy on Truce?

You can buy from a list of 120+ fruits and vegetables products on the app.

How do I use the app?

In this app, you can create an order for one or more products by selecting products and adding quantity.

What happens after I place
an order?

After you place an order on the app, Truce team immediately verifies the order and then starts identifying suitable sellers for your requirement. After we receive quotes from sellers for your order, you will be notified with selected set of sellers.

What is the benefit for me to buy products
on Truce?

Truce will provide you access to farmers across India, without having to travel to different place. Thus you can compare prices from farmers from all over India and decide, and save money. All the sellers on the platform are also verified by Truce field team.

Who is the seller?

A seller is farmer or a local agent near farming regions.

How to know the status of
my order?

You can login into the Truce app and check orders page for the status of your order.

Who will manage the
transport/logistics?

We help in facilitating a complete transaction, and also share regular udpates on your order status.
