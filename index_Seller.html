What is Truce?

Truce is an online B2B managed marketplace for fruits and vegetables. We help producers of fruits and vegetables reach out to wholesale buyers across India to get better price for their products.

Who is Truce app for?

Truce Seller app is for producers and wholesalers looking to sell fruits and vegetables to verified wholesale buyers across India.

How do I use the app?

In this app, you can create your online shop by posting deals of your products. These deals will be visible to buyers across India and you will get order requests directly. On the order requests, add your quote price and get order confirmation.

What all can I sell on Truce.

You can sell from a list of 120+ fruits and vegetables products on the app.

What is the benefit for me to sell my products on Truce?

Truce will help you get better price for your products than your current market. We manage the transactions end-to-end and have a verified buyer base across India.

Who is the buyer?

A buyer can be a wholesaler or trader of fruits or vegetables from anywhere in India. Truce being an online marketplace, you can sell to buyers from anywhere in India without the need to physically meet them.

How to know the status of
my quote?

In the app, on the orders page you can get to know the status of your quote. Remember to put best price, so that buyer can select your quote easily.

How is the payment
managed?

Truce ensures payment guarantee for all its sellers, provided the desired quality is delivered by the seller. You can talk more to our expert on the app about the payment based on the transaction.

Where are the price trends shown on the app gathered from?

The price trends shown on the app are gathered from various fruits and vegetable markets across India on a daily basis.

How do I increase my chances of getting
more orders?

Build your online shop by posting deals with good photos to increase your chances. Also ensure to respond with competitive prices for order requests.
